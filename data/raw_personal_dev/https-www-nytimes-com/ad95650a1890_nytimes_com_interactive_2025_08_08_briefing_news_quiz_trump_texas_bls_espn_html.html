The New York Times News Quiz, Aug. 8, 2024

Aug. 8, 2025

Did you follow the news this week? Take our quiz to see how well you stack up with other Times readers.

1 of 11

Democratic lawmakers in Texas left the state to prevent the Legislature from taking a vote on what?

2 of 11

President Trump fired Erika McEntarfer, commissioner of the Bureau of Labor Statistics, after her agency released a jobs report. What did he say was his reason?

3 of 11

Law enforcement officers spent the week searching a rugged forest for a man suspected of killing four people in a bar in which state?

4 of 11

Health Secretary Robert F. Kennedy Jr. this week canceled $500 million in contracts and grants to research which medical technology?

5 of 11

Apple’s chief executive announced that the company would invest $100 billion in the U.S., after he presented Trump a 24-karat gold gift. What’s the chief executive’s name?

6 of 11

Which actor, known for playing Superman, announced this week that he was becoming an Immigration and Customs Enforcement officer?

7 of 11

Which professional sports league struck a deal to own a 10 percent stake in ESPN?

8 of 11

Sean Duffy, the acting NASA administrator, announced that he was prioritizing an effort to build what on the moon?

9 of 11

Trump said this week that he would double tariffs on India, to 50 percent, as punishment for its continued purchase of oil from which country?

10 of 11

Which of these movies — a sequel to a popular 2003 comedy — is hitting theaters this week?

11 of 11

A zoo in Denmark recently asked members of the public to donate what to its lions and lynxes?

Tom Wright-Piersanti, Lyna Bentahar and the staff of the Morning newsletter compiled this week’s quiz.
