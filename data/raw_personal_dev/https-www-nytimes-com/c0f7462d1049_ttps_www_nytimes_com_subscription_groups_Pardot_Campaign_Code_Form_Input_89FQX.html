Frequently Asked Questions

  • + What is a group subscription?

    A group subscription provides your organization with unlimited digital access to The New York Times via any device. Account support is included.

  • + Is there a minimum number of users required?

    Yes, there is a minimum number of users. The minimum will vary per account type.

  • + What is the cost and duration of the program?

    Pricing depends on the number of users who will have access, plus any program customization. A subscription is for 52 weeks.

  • + What payment options are available?

    Group subscriptions are billed annually as one invoice and can be paid via credit card, direct debit, check or electronic bank payment.

  • + How soon is access granted?

    7-10 business days, in most cases.

  • + Is the print edition of The New York Times included?

    The print edition of The New York Times is not included as part of a digital group subscription or site license. Multi-copy print subscriptions may be purchased by reaching out to businesscare@nytimes.com or calling 1-800-753-7795

  • + How can I obtain permission to republish journalism from The New York Times?

    Through the group subscription program, you have the option to consult with The New York Times Licensing Group to obtain permission to republish our quality journalism at an exceptional value.
