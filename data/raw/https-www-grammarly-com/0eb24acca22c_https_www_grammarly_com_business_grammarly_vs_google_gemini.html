Frequently Asked Questions

Is Grammarly better than Gemini for Google Workspace?

Both Grammarly and Gemini provide AI writing assistance but serve different needs. Grammarly is specifically designed for workplace communication, offering advanced team features and integrating with over 500,000 sites and apps for consistent writing support. While Gemini is built into Google Workspace, Grammarly‘s broader compatibility and focus on productivity make it a better choice for teams working across multiple tools.

What is Grammarly used for?

Grammarly helps professionals and teams make every document and message more effective. Craft polished emails, add clarity to documents, and overcome writer’s block with advanced AI technology and extensive integrations designed to speed up the writing process and mitigate miscommunication.

Can you use Grammarly within Google products?

Yes, Grammarly works seamlessly in various Google Workspace apps. It integrates with Google Docs through our browser extension, available for Chrome and other popular browsers. Additionally, Grammarly works in Gmail and Google Sheets via our desktop app for both Windows and Mac.

How does Grammarly use my data?

Grammarly collects necessary information, such as username, email address, and contact and language preferences. It also processes your text to deliver personalized writing feedback, but we adhere to stringent security mechanisms to protect your data and maintain your privacy. Grammarly does not and will not sell your data. Read more in our Privacy Policy.
