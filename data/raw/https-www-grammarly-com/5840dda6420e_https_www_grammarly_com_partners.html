Today’s teams are stuck in a cycle of updates, distractions, and inefficiencies. But the ones who know how to leverage AI? They’re breaking free. Grammarly and Coda help put AI to work for modern teams— with results you and your customers will see.

A trusted name in communication AI

Grammarly is an industry leading AI assistant with enterprise-grade credibility. It solves for a universal pain point, unclear communication, and with proven ROI and high engagement, it's a win for you and your customers.

All-in-one collaboration, centralized peace of mind

Coda brings documents, data, and workflows into one flexible platform. It’s a natural fit for teams looking to streamline tools and boost productivity—with stickiness that drives retention.

Lead AI change and grow your brand

As AI reshapes the way people work, customers aren't just looking for tools—they're looking for guidance. As a Grammarly and Coda partner, you can lead change initiatives and unlock more services opportunities along the way.

Grammarly and Coda are tried, trusted, and team-approved

Grammarly works on 500,000+ apps and websites so you can help your customers realize better communication wherever their work happens.
Coda acts as a single source of truth, with 600+ integrations across your customers' favorite tools.

Unlock opportunities in specialized markets

Grammarly can help you deliver deeper value to your customers, whether you're supporting a specialized sector like education or improving a core function like customer experience.

Transparent AI for educators and students

Grammarly’s new Authorship feature opens the door to high-impact conversations with institutions navigating AI in education. It helps your education customers promote academic integrity while giving you a powerful, timely solution to drive new revenue and deepen relationships in this regulated and AI-wary space.

Quality-first AI for customer-facing teams

Support quality is slipping, and CX leaders are under pressure to fix it fast. With Grammarly, you can offer a proven solution that helps customer experience teams save time, protect brand voice, and improve satisfaction—while unlocking high-value, high-retention opportunities in a growth-focused market.
