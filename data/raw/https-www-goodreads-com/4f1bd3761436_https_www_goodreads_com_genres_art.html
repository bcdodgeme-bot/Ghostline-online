    Updated in 2025, the second edition of Fiddle Studio Book 1 fixes errors, updates links and product info, and adds even more easy, beginner fiddle tunes.

    Learn the fiddle from scratch from setting up to play and holding the instrument to playing easy tunes. In this book Megan Wobus Beller, the founder of Fiddle Studio, takes the tunes and techniques from a whole year of fiddle lessons and puts it all in one place.

    It’s the information you need to start with no experience, and you can reference it again and again. Meg covers the common questions and concerns and answers with her expert perspective from 25 years of teaching.

    Over 30 fiddle tunes are organized in an intuitive order that builds new skills one at a time. Each tune is presented first with fingerings and strings, and then in standard notation with chords.

    You do not need to read music to learn the tunes in this book!

    Book 1 topics (links and prices updated in 2nd edition): fiddle, bow, accessories, tapes, rosin, tuning, bowgrip, set up, hand position, bowing, reading, high and low fingers, tension, the pinky, highway, hitting the right string, playing by ear, singing, string care, listening, playlists, the mirror, mutes, finger care, slow practice, writing in, teacher ears, breaks, playing with others, sessions per week, and planning practice.

    D Major Modal: Bill Harte's Jig

    Plus: St. Antoine's Reel, and All the Rage
