    Katie Jantz, BSc Pharmacy, MSc Human Nutrition

    Bio

    Katie is a registered pharmacist with a master’s degree in human nutrition and functional medicine. Curious from a young age, she loves deepening her understanding of the human body and the ways in which we can influence health. She is passionate about science communication as a means of empowering others to take charge of their own health. Katie lives in the prairies of Canada and enjoys time spent in nature, a good book, and trying to master the art of fermented foods.

    • MSc. Human Nutrition, University of Western States
