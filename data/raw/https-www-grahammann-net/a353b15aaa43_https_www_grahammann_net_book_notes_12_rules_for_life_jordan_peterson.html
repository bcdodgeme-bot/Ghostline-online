The main problem I have with it is the poor flow. Â This seems to be a result of a few things:

  1. The book feels disjointed - there should be more than 12 rules, and more sections, and the book would be much clearer.
  2. At the same time, many sections feel meandering, and the connections that Peterson tries to make often donât make sense to me, to the point that I have to re-read repeatedly, and ultimately just make me frustrated.
