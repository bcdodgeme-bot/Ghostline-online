Coaching nutrition is about more than
macronutrient splits and meal plans.

As a coach, you work with real people, dealing with real situations, real struggles with change, all within the messiness of real life…

  • People who wake up in the morning determined to eat healthy but find themselves raiding the snack cabinet in a moment of emotional distress just a few hours later.
  • People who want to improve their habits and make a change, but continually struggle.
  • People who may even know what they need to dothey just cant seem to do it consistently.

If you cant help people overcome these real life challenges, youll struggle to feel confident that you can help your clients get the results they truly want (and deserve). And your clients will feel stuck in the cycle of quick fixes that give them temporary hopewithout lasting change.

This is why great coaches understand the complexities of how behavior and change works and why, so they can move people toward their goalseven when motivation and inspiration fall short.

How Do We Change Our Behavior is a deep-dive into understanding why we battle change, even when its what we want most, and how you can sharpen your change-making skills to help your clients overcome obstacles and move forward in a healthy direction.

How the course works

How Do We Change Our Behavior is a self-paced, digital course thats broken down into two carefully-crafted units that cover both the science and practical application of behavior-change psychology. Heres an overview of whats covered in each unit:

Unit 1 – Understanding Behavior

Estimated learning time: 10-12 hrs

You’ll learn what behavior is and where it comes from. This unit takes a biopsychosocial, deep health perspective and explores how both behavior and change result from complex interactions between our body, brain, and environment.

Building on these ideas, you’ll learn the deep change concept of sustainable, wide-ranging growth and evolution (rather than hacks or quick fixes).

Unit 2 – Change in Practice

Estimated learning time: 10-12 hrs

You’ll go deeper into how change happens and how you can facilitate it with clients to produce incredible results. Youll get specific skills, practices, and actions to support growth and understand why these particular techniques are most effective.

Included is PN’s Change Predictor Assessment Tool, with advanced features to assess your clients’ readiness to change, helping you develop a plan that will have the most chance of success.
