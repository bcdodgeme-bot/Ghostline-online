ABOUT IMPOSTOR SYNDROME INSTITUTE™

Our mission is to address the (avoidable) impact of impostor syndrome on individuals and organizations through Humble Realist™ thinking. 

 

Impostor Syndrome Institute (ISI) is the official provider of information, insight and tools to organizations and individuals since 1983.

 

ISI was co-founded by global thought leader Dr. Valerie Young, widely recognized as the foremost expert on impostor syndrome, and Carolyn Herfurth, a leading business strategist with experience that cuts across Fortune 500s, mid-sized businesses and independents such as speakers, thought leaders and small business owners.

 

We help coaches, speakers, and organizations through our Impostor Syndrome Informed-Coach™ program, speaker licensing, facilitator training for organizations, and more. 

 

Our strong commitment to diversity, inclusion, and equity in all forms is woven throughout all of our solutions.
