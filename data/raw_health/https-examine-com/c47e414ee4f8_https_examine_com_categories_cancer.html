    Cancer

    Written by:
    Kamal Patel

    Fact-checked

    by:

    Louie Neri, Zia Poonja
    •Last Updated: August 16, 2022

    Cancer is a group of diseases characterized by cells growing and replicating uncontrollably. Damage to DNA is central to the development of cancerous cells. No diet or supplement has been shown to cure cancer, but some diets and supplements may affect prognosis, symptoms, and treatment.

    Does CBT reduce the fear of breast cancer recurrence?New
    August 8, 2025/Women’s Health•Cancer•Breast Cancer

    In this meta-analysis of 14 randomized controlled trials in over 2,000 women with breast cancer, cognitive behavioral therapy (CBT) reduced the fear of cancer recurrence only when the therapy was guided by a therapist or was provided face to face.
