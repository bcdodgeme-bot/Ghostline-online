ECHO Cohort Study Sites

ECHO Cohort Study Sites

ECHO Cohort Study Sites intend to increase the number of child and adolescent participants from diverse racial, geographic, and socioeconomic backgrounds in the ECHO Cohort to more than 60,000 by May 2030. These sites will lead collaborative ECHO Cohort science; follow existing ECHO Cohort participants; recruit new pregnant participants from diverse populations along with their offspring offspring, and, if available, the conceiving partner; develop and implement the ECHO Cohort Preconception Pilot Study; and implement the ECHO Cohort Data and Biospecimen Collection Protocol. Each study site collaborates with others to combine data that are collected in a standardized way across the consortium.
