Office of Legislative Policy and Analysis (OLPA)

Image

The Office of Legislative Policy and Analysis (OLPA) provides essential information, advice, and guidance on Congressional actions affecting NIH to the NIH Director, the Office of the Director, NIH’s Institutes and Centers, and the broader NIH community.
