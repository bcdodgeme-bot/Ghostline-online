Public Information

Public Information

  • Leads design, content management, usability and analytics for the NIH website.
  • Sets policies for best web practices. Provides guidance and strategic coordination across NIH.
  • Serves as point of contact for online information for the Department of Health and Human Services (HHS) and other federal agencies.
  • Oversees NIH social media strategy, messaging and events.
  • Manages NIH’s Health Information portal and NIH's Spanish Language Health Information portal, Salud NIH, which features Pregúntele a Carla, a monthly Spanish-language column on health topics.
  • Distributes news and information through the following:
    • NIH News in Health, a monthly newsletter offering practical health information based on NIH-supported research.
    • NIH Record, a biweekly newsletter covering issues of significance to NIH staff, contractors, and trainees.
    • NIH Research Matters, a weekly update of NIH research highlights.
    • Your Healthiest Self, toolkits on how to improve overall health and well-being.
