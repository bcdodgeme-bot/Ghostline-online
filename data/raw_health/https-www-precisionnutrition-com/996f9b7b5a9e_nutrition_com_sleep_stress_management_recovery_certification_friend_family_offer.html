What grade do I need to pass?

At least 75%, which most students easily achieve or surpass.

What if I fail an exam?

You can’t fail a specific exam. Your score for the course is cumulative. So you could do very poorly on a few exams and still pass the course as long as you answer 210 out of 280 questions by the end of the course.

What if I don’t pass the course?

If you don’t earn 210 out of 280 points in the course, you can take a re-do exam at the end of the course.
