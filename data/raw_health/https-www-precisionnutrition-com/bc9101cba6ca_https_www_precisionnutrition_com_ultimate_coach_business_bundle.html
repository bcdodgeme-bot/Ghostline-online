What does “self-paced” mean for AI Bootcamp?

Unlike the live version of this class we released in November 2023, this course gives you access to all 8 weeks of class and lesson content—in a format that allows you to work through the course at your own pace and schedule.

With each lesson, you’ll have access to:

  • a recorded instructed lesson
  • supplemental reading to help you get the most from the recorded sessions
  • links to resources shared in the session
  • A “challenge”—an activity that goes along with what you’re learning, helping you to put your new knowledge into practice

In addition to the lessons, you have access to our private community—a private group that provides space to discuss, learn, and explore the course material and specific scenarios, and get feedback and support from your peers.
