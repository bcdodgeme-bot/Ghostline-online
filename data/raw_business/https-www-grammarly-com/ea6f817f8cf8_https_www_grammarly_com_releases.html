Improve collaboration and prove the impact

Working with Grammarly and Coda is an investment in your team, and now you can track how that investment affects your org’s KPIs.

Effective Communication Score

The Effective Communication Score, now generally available, tracks the overall strength of your organization’s communication and shows key metrics like brand consistency, inclusivity, clarity, and correctness so you can celebrate progress and identify areas for improvement.

ROI report

Your ROI report card provides concrete evidence for how Grammarly improves your team’s writing and impacts your organization’s unique metrics and goals.

AI usage analytics

Get more insights into what tools and activities are driving productivity. Now, you can explore usage trends across Coda docs, and admins can view workspace consumption over time.

Enterprise doc analytics dashboard

Enjoy a centralized Coda dashboard with high-level stats and detailed doc info. Admins can manage their organization’s content with easy sorting, filtering, and permission settings all in one place.

Updates for account admins to scale more productive workdays

AI only works if it adapts as your business grows. We’ve made key updates to your account hub to help future-proof our work together.

Feature management hub

Get everything you need—and nothing you don’t. Now, admins can control which features are enabled for the organization.

Account hub navigation enhancements

We’ve given your account hub a makeover to make it easier to navigate. Admins can now use bookmarks, search, and more to manage their team’s Grammarly account.

Folder permissions

Your Coda workspace just got more secure. Share folders with view, comment, or edit access, and all docs in the folder will inherit permissions.

Sync page access control

Now, you can sync a specific page into another doc without granting full access to the source doc, giving collaborators necessary context while keeping select information secure and private.
